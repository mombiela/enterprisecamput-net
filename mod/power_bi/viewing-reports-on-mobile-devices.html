Introduction

Power BI is a powerful tool for data visualization and business intelligence. One of its key features is the ability to view and interact with reports on mobile devices. This section will guide you through the process of accessing and using Power BI reports on mobile devices, from basic setup to advanced features.

Setting Up Power BI on Mobile Devices

To get started with viewing Power BI reports on your mobile device, you need to install the Power BI mobile app.

Installing the Power BI Mobile App

  • For iOS Devices:

    1. Open the App Store.
    2. Search for "Power BI".
    3. Tap "Get" to download and install the app.

  • For Android Devices:

    1. Open the Google Play Store.
    2. Search for "Power BI".
    3. Tap "Install" to download and install the app.

Logging In

  • Open the Power BI app.
  • Enter your Power BI account credentials (email and password).
  • If you use multi-factor authentication, complete the additional verification steps.

Navigating the Power BI Mobile App

Once logged in, you will see the main dashboard. Here are the key components:

  • Home: Displays your most frequently used reports and dashboards.
  • Favorites: Quick access to your favorite reports.
  • Shared with Me: Reports and dashboards shared with you by others.
  • Apps: Access to organizational apps that contain collections of related dashboards and reports.

Viewing Reports

Accessing Reports

  • Tap on a report from the Home screen or navigate through the Favorites or Shared with Me sections.
  • Use the search bar to find specific reports.

Interacting with Reports

  • Zooming and Panning: Pinch to zoom in and out. Drag to pan across the report.
  • Filtering Data: Tap on visual elements to apply filters.
  • Drill-Through: Tap on data points to drill through to more detailed views.

Example: Interacting with a Sales Report

1. Open the Sales Report from the Home screen.
2. Pinch to zoom into the sales by region chart.
3. Tap on the "North America" region to filter the entire report by this region.
4. Observe how other visuals update to reflect the filtered data.

Advanced Features

Offline Viewing

  • Enable Offline Mode: Download reports for offline viewing.
    1. Open the report you want to view offline.
    2. Tap the ellipsis (...) in the top right corner.
    3. Select "Make available offline".

Annotations and Sharing

  • Adding Annotations:

    1. Open the report you want to annotate.
    2. Tap the pencil icon to enter annotation mode.
    3. Draw or write directly on the report.
    4. Save the annotated report.

  • Sharing Reports:

    1. Open the report you want to share.
    2. Tap the share icon.
    3. Choose the sharing method (email, link, etc.).
    4. Enter the recipient's details and send.

Summary

Viewing and interacting with Power BI reports on mobile devices is a convenient way to stay connected with your data on the go. By installing the Power BI mobile app, logging in, and navigating through the various sections, you can easily access and interact with your reports. Advanced features like offline viewing and annotations further enhance the mobile experience. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced user, leveraging Power BI on mobile devices can significantly improve your data accessibility and decision-making processes.
