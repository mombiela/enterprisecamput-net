Publishing to App Stores
Continuous Integration and Delivery ►

Publishing your Ionic application to app stores is a crucial step to make your app available to users. This section will guide you through the process of preparing, building, and submitting your Ionic app to both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

Preparing Your App for Release

Before you can publish your app, you need to ensure it meets the requirements of the app stores.

  • App Store Guidelines: Familiarize yourself with the guidelines of the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

    • Apple App Store Guidelines
    • Google Play Store Guidelines

  • App Icons and Splash Screens: Ensure your app has the necessary icons and splash screens for different device resolutions.

    • Use the Ionic CLI to generate resources: 
      ionic cordova resources

  • App Versioning: Update your app version in config.xml:

    <widget id="com.example.app" version="1.0.0" ...>

  • Testing: Thoroughly test your app on real devices to ensure it works as expected.

Building Your App

Building for iOS

To build your Ionic app for iOS, you need a Mac with Xcode installed.

  1. Add the iOS Platform:

    ionic cordova platform add ios

  2. Build the App:

    ionic cordova build ios --prod --release

  3. Open the Project in Xcode:

    open platforms/ios/YourApp.xcworkspace

  4. Configure Signing and Capabilities:

    • In Xcode, select your project and navigate to the "Signing & Capabilities" tab.
    • Ensure you have a valid Apple Developer account and configure the necessary signing certificates and provisioning profiles.

  5. Archive and Export:

    • In Xcode, select "Product" > "Archive".
    • Once the archive is created, click "Distribute App" and follow the prompts to upload your app to the App Store.

Building for Android

To build your Ionic app for Android, you need Android Studio installed.

  1. Add the Android Platform:

    ionic cordova platform add android

  2. Build the App:

    ionic cordova build android --prod --release

  3. Sign the APK:

    • Generate a keystore if you don't have one: 
      keytool -genkey -v -keystore my-release-key.keystore -alias my-key-alias -keyalg RSA -keysize 2048 -validity 10000
    • Sign the APK: 
      jarsigner -verbose -sigalg SHA1withRSA -digestalg SHA1 -keystore my-release-key.keystore platforms/android/app/build/outputs/apk/release/app-release-unsigned.apk my-key-alias
    • Optimize the APK: 
      zipalign -v 4 platforms/android/app/build/outputs/apk/release/app-release-unsigned.apk MyApp.apk

Submitting Your App

Submitting to the Apple App Store

  1. Create an App Store Connect Account: If you don't have one, create an account at App Store Connect.

  2. Create a New App:

    • Log in to App Store Connect and click the "+" button to create a new app.
    • Fill in the required information, such as app name, primary language, and bundle ID.

  3. Upload Your Build:

    • In Xcode, after archiving your app, select "Distribute App" and choose "App Store Connect".
    • Follow the prompts to upload your build.

  4. Submit for Review:

    • Once your build is uploaded, go back to App Store Connect and complete the app information, pricing, and availability.
    • Submit your app for review.

Submitting to the Google Play Store

  1. Create a Google Play Developer Account: If you don't have one, create an account at Google Play Console.

  2. Create a New App:

    • Log in to Google Play Console and click "Create App".
    • Fill in the required information, such as app name, default language, and app category.

  3. Upload Your APK:

    • Navigate to the "Release" section and create a new release.
    • Upload your signed APK and provide the necessary release notes.

  4. Complete Store Listing:

    • Fill in the app details, including description, screenshots, and promotional graphics.

  5. Submit for Review:

    • Once all information is complete, review your app and submit it for review.

Conclusion

Publishing your Ionic app to the Apple App Store and Google Play Store involves several steps, from preparing your app to building and finally submitting it for review. By following the guidelines and steps outlined in this section, you can ensure a smooth publishing process and make your app available to a wide audience. Remember to thoroughly test your app and adhere to the app store guidelines to avoid any rejections. Happy publishing!
