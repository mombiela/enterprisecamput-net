Introduction

Job management in Control Language (CL) is crucial for handling tasks and processes efficiently on IBM i systems. This topic covers the essential commands for managing jobs, from basic to advanced levels.

Basic Job Management Commands

Submitting Jobs

  • SBMJOB (Submit Job): This command is used to submit a job to a job queue. 
    SBMJOB CMD(CALL PGM(MYPGM)) JOB(MYJOB)
    • CMD: Specifies the command to run.
    • JOB: Names the job.

Displaying Job Status

  • WRKACTJOB (Work with Active Jobs): Displays a list of active jobs. 
    WRKACTJOB
    • This command shows the status, CPU usage, and other details of active jobs.

Holding and Releasing Jobs

  • HLDJOB (Hold Job): Holds a job, preventing it from running. 
    HLDJOB JOB(123456/MYUSER/MYJOB)
    • JOB: Specifies the job to hold.
  • RLSJOB (Release Job): Releases a held job. 
    RLSJOB JOB(123456/MYUSER/MYJOB)
    • JOB: Specifies the job to release.

Intermediate Job Management Commands

Ending Jobs

  • ENDJOB (End Job): Ends a job immediately or after a specified delay. 
    ENDJOB JOB(123456/MYUSER/MYJOB) OPTION(*IMMED)
    • OPTION(*IMMED): Ends the job immediately.
    • OPTION(*CNTRLD): Ends the job in a controlled manner, allowing it to clean up.

Changing Job Attributes

  • CHGJOB (Change Job): Changes the attributes of a job. 
    CHGJOB JOB(123456/MYUSER/MYJOB) RUNPTY(20)
    • RUNPTY: Specifies the run priority of the job.

Job Logs

  • DSPJOBLOG (Display Job Log): Displays the job log for a specified job. 
    DSPJOBLOG JOB(123456/MYUSER/MYJOB)
    • JOB: Specifies the job whose log you want to display.

Advanced Job Management Commands

Job Queues

  • WRKJOBQ (Work with Job Queues): Manages job queues. 
    WRKJOBQ JOBQ(QBATCH)
    • JOBQ: Specifies the job queue to work with.

Job Scheduling

  • ADDJOBSCDE (Add Job Schedule Entry): Adds a job to the job scheduler. 
    ADDJOBSCDE JOB(MYJOB) CMD(CALL PGM(MYPGM)) FRQ(*WEEKLY) SCDDATE(*NONE) SCDTIME(120000)
    • FRQ: Specifies the frequency of the job.
    • SCDDATE: Specifies the scheduled date.
    • SCDTIME: Specifies the scheduled time.

Job Descriptions

  • CRTJOBD (Create Job Description): Creates a job description. 
    CRTJOBD JOBD(MYJOBD) JOBQ(QBATCH) RQSDTA('CALL PGM(MYPGM)')
    • JOBD: Specifies the job description name.
    • JOBQ: Specifies the job queue.
    • RQSDTA: Specifies the request data.

Summary

Job management in CL involves a variety of commands to submit, monitor, control, and schedule jobs. Understanding these commands is essential for efficient system operations. From basic commands like SBMJOB and WRKACTJOB to advanced commands like ADDJOBSCDE and CRTJOBD, mastering these tools will enable you to manage jobs effectively on IBM i systems.
