Introduction

In this section, you will put into practice the concepts learned about keyword research. This exercise will help you identify relevant keywords for a new project, using keyword research tools and techniques.

Exercise Objectives

  • Apply keyword research techniques.
  • Use keyword research tools.
  • Analyze and select relevant keywords for a specific project.

Step 1: Define the Project

Before starting keyword research, it is essential to have a clear understanding of the project. Answer the following questions:

  • What is the main objective of the project?
  • Who is the target audience?
  • What type of content will be created?

Example:

Project: Blog about healthy recipes.

  • Objective: Attract people interested in healthy eating.
  • Target audience: Adults aged 25-45 interested in cooking and health.
  • Type of content: Recipes, nutrition tips, articles about healthy ingredients.

Step 2: Generate an Initial List of Keywords

Start by generating an initial list of keywords related to the project. Use your knowledge of the topic and keyword suggestion tools like Google Keyword Planner, Ubersuggest, or AnswerThePublic.

Example:

  • Healthy recipes
  • Healthy food
  • Nutritious breakfasts
  • Low-calorie lunches
  • Light dinners

Step 3: Use Keyword Research Tools

Use keyword research tools to expand and refine your initial list. Some recommended tools include:

  • Google Keyword Planner: Provides data on search volume and keyword competition.
  • Ubersuggest: Offers keyword suggestions and competition analysis.
  • AnswerThePublic: Generates questions and phrases related to your keywords.

Example with Google Keyword Planner:

  1. Go to Google Keyword Planner.
  2. Enter one of your initial keywords, such as "healthy recipes."
  3. Review the suggestions and search volume data.

| Keyword | Monthly Search Volume | Competition | |-----------------------|-----------------------|-------------| | Healthy recipes | 10,000 | Medium | | Healthy food | 8,000 | High | | Nutritious breakfasts | 5,000 | Low | | Low-calorie lunches | 3,000 | Medium | | Light dinners | 4,000 | Low |

Step 4: Analyze and Select Keywords

Analyze the data obtained and select the most relevant keywords with a suitable balance between search volume and competition.

Selection Criteria:

  • Keywords with high search volume and low competition are ideal.
  • Consider relevance to your target audience and the content you plan to create.

Final Selection:

  • Healthy recipes
  • Nutritious breakfasts
  • Light dinners

Step 5: Create Content Based on Keywords

Use the selected keywords to create optimized content. Make sure to include the keywords in:

  • Titles and subtitles
  • Introduction and conclusion
  • Meta descriptions and image alt tags

Example of Title and Meta Description:

<title>Healthy Recipes for Every Meal of the Day</title>
<meta name="description" content="Discover healthy and delicious recipes for nutritious breakfasts, low-calorie lunches, and light dinners.">

Conclusion

Keyword research is a fundamental process for any content project. Through this exercise, you have learned to define a project, generate an initial list of keywords, use research tools, analyze data, and select relevant keywords. With this foundation, you will be better prepared to create optimized content that attracts your target audience and improves your visibility in search engines.
