Introduction

The preparation of reports and the presentation of results are crucial skills for a Community Manager. These reports allow for the evaluation of digital marketing strategies' performance, identification of areas for improvement, and communication of results to stakeholders.

Module Objectives

  • Understand the importance of reports in Community Management.
  • Learn to collect and analyze relevant data.
  • Develop skills to present results effectively.

Types of Reports

Monthly Reports

  • Objective: Evaluate the performance of strategies over the month.
  • Content:
    • Key metrics (KPI) such as reach, interaction, and community growth.
    • Content analysis: most and least effective posts.
    • Recommendations for the next month.

Campaign Reports

  • Objective: Evaluate the success of a specific campaign.
  • Content:
    • Campaign objectives.
    • Results obtained vs. stated objectives.
    • ROI (Return on Investment).
    • Lessons learned and recommendations.

Crisis Reports

  • Objective: Document and analyze crisis management.
  • Content:
    • Description of the crisis.
    • Actions taken.
    • Results and management analysis.
    • Action plan for future crises.

Data Collection and Analysis

Analysis Tools

  • Google Analytics: To analyze web traffic and user behavior.
  • Hootsuite Analytics: To measure social media performance.
  • Facebook Insights: To obtain specific data from Facebook.
  • Twitter Analytics: To analyze performance on Twitter.

Key Metrics (KPI)

  • Reach: Number of people who have seen your content.
  • Interaction: Number of likes, comments, shares, etc.
  • Community Growth: New followers or subscribers.
  • CTR (Click-Through Rate): Percentage of clicks on links.
  • Engagement Rate: Total interaction divided by the number of followers.

Results Presentation

Report Structure

  1. Introduction: Context and objectives of the report.
  2. Methodology: Tools and methods used to collect data.
  3. Results: Presentation of the collected data.
  4. Analysis: Interpretation of the results.
  5. Conclusions and Recommendations: Suggested actions based on the analysis.

Tools for Presenting Results

  • Microsoft PowerPoint: To create visual presentations.
  • Google Slides: Online alternative for collaborative presentations.
  • Canva: To design attractive graphics and visuals.
  • Tableau: To create interactive dashboards.

Report Example

# Monthly Social Media Report - September 2023

## Introduction
This report analyzes the performance of our social media during the month of September 2023.

## Methodology
The data was collected using Hootsuite Analytics and Facebook Insights.

## Results
- **Total Reach**: 150,000 people
- **Total Interaction**: 20,000 interactions
- **Community Growth**: 500 new followers

## Analysis
Reach increased by 10% compared to the previous month, mainly due to the product promotion campaign.

## Conclusions and Recommendations
- Continue with the product promotion strategy.
- Increase the frequency of posts on Instagram, where higher interaction was observed.

Conclusion

The preparation of reports and the presentation of results are essential for measuring the success of Community Management strategies. By using the appropriate tools and methods, Community Managers can provide valuable information and make informed decisions to continuously improve their strategies.
