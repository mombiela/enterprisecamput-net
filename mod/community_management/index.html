Module 1: Introduction to Community Management

  • What is Community Management?
  • Importance of Community Management in the digital strategy
  • Roles and responsibilities of the Community Manager

Module 2: Knowing the Community

  • Identification and segmentation of the audience
  • Creation of user profiles (Buyer Personas)
  • Analysis of community behavior and needs

Module 3: Content Strategies

  • Planning and content calendar
  • Types of content for social networks
  • Creation of attractive and relevant content
  • Use of content management tools

Module 4: Interaction Management

  • Handling comments and messages
  • Strategies to encourage participation and engagement
  • Crisis management and negative comments
  • Community moderation

Module 5: Tools and Platforms

  • Main social networks and their characteristics
  • Monitoring and analysis tools
  • Automation and scheduling of posts
  • Use of chatbots and virtual assistants

Module 6: Analysis and Reports

  • Key metrics for Community Management
  • Data analysis and generation of insights
  • Preparation of reports and presentation of results
  • Adjustment of strategies based on analysis

Module 7: Practical Cases and Exercises

  • Case studies of success in Community Management
  • Practical exercises in interaction management
  • Crisis management simulation
  • Creation of a Community Management plan

Module 8: Trends and Future of Community Management

  • New trends in social networks
  • The future of Community Management
  • Adaptation to changes in user behavior
  • Technological innovations and their impact on community management

This outline provides a complete and detailed structure for a Community Management course, covering from basic concepts to future trends, through practical strategies and essential tools.
