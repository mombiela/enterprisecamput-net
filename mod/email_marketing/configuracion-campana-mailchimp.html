Introduction

Mailchimp is one of the most popular tools for managing email marketing campaigns. In this section, you will learn how to set up a campaign in Mailchimp from scratch, including creating contact lists, designing emails, and scheduling sends.

Step 1: Create an Account in Mailchimp

Before setting up a campaign, you need an account in Mailchimp.

  • Visit Mailchimp.
  • Click on "Sign Up Free".
  • Complete the registration form with your email, username, and password.
  • Verify your email to activate the account.

Step 2: Create a Contact List

A contact list is essential for any email marketing campaign.

  1. Log in to your Mailchimp account.
  2. Navigate to the "Audience" section.
  3. Click on "Create Audience".
  4. Complete the audience details:
    • Audience name.
    • Sender email address.
    • Sender name.
    • Reminder of how contacts joined your list.

Step 3: Import Contacts

You can add contacts manually or import an existing list.

  • Add contacts manually:

    1. Go to "Audience".
    2. Select "Add a subscriber".
    3. Complete the required fields (name, email, etc.).

  • Import a contact list:

    1. Go to "Audience".
    2. Select "Import contacts".
    3. Choose the import method (CSV file, copy and paste, etc.).
    4. Upload your file and map the fields.

Step 4: Create a Campaign

Now that you have a contact list, you can create your campaign.

  1. Navigate to the "Campaigns" section.
  2. Click on "Create Campaign".
  3. Select "Email".
  4. Complete the campaign details:
    • Campaign name.
    • Campaign type (Regular, Automated, Plain-text, etc.).

Step 5: Design the Email

Mailchimp offers various templates and a drag-and-drop editor to design your email.

  1. Select a template:

    • Pre-designed templates.
    • Saved templates.
    • Blank templates.

  2. Use the drag-and-drop editor:

    • Add content blocks (text, images, buttons, etc.).
    • Customize the design (colors, fonts, etc.).

HTML Code Example

If you prefer to design your email with HTML, you can do so in the code section.

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
<head>
  <title>My Campaign</title>
</head>
<body>
  <h1>Welcome to our Newsletter</h1>
  <p>Thank you for subscribing to our updates.</p>
  <a href="https://yourwebsite.com">Visit our website</a>
</body>
</html>

Step 6: Configure Sending Details

Before sending your campaign, you need to configure some important details.

  • Recipients: Select the contact list to which you will send the campaign.
  • Sender: Verify the sender's email address and name.
  • Subject: Write an attractive subject for your email.

Step 7: Schedule or Send the Campaign

You can send the campaign immediately or schedule it for a specific date and time.

  • Send now:

    1. Click on "Send".
    2. Confirm the send.

  • Schedule send:

    1. Click on "Schedule".
    2. Select the date and time.
    3. Confirm the scheduling.

Conclusion

Setting up a campaign in Mailchimp is a straightforward process that involves creating an account, setting up a contact list, designing the email, and scheduling the send. By following these steps, you can launch effective email marketing campaigns and reach your audience efficiently.
