Software and Tools for Strategic Analysis

Next ►

In today's fast-paced business environment, leveraging software and tools for strategic analysis can significantly enhance the accuracy and efficiency of your strategic planning process. This section will cover various software and tools that can assist in conducting comprehensive strategic analyses, including PESTEL, SWOT, and Porter's Five Forces analyses, as well as tools for monitoring and controlling strategy implementation.

  1. PESTEL Analysis Tools

a. PESTLE Analysis

Description: PESTLE Analysis is a dedicated tool for conducting PESTEL analyses. It helps in identifying and analyzing the Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Environmental, and Legal factors that impact an organization.

Features:

  • Predefined templates for PESTEL analysis.
  • Customizable factors and sub-factors.
  • Visual representation of analysis results.
  • Export options for reports.

Example:

Political: Government policies, trade tariffs.
Economic: Inflation rate, economic growth.
Social: Demographic changes, cultural trends.
Technological: Technological advancements, R&D activity.
Environmental: Climate change, sustainability initiatives.
Legal: Employment laws, health and safety regulations.

b. MindTools

Description: MindTools offers a comprehensive suite of tools for strategic analysis, including PESTEL analysis. It provides step-by-step guides and templates to facilitate the analysis process.

Features:

  • Interactive templates for PESTEL analysis.
  • Educational resources and guides.
  • Integration with other strategic analysis tools.
  • User-friendly interface.

  1. SWOT Analysis Tools

a. SWOT Manager

Description: SWOT Manager is a specialized tool for conducting SWOT (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats) analysis. It helps in systematically identifying and evaluating internal and external factors affecting an organization.

Features:

  • Structured templates for SWOT analysis.
  • Collaboration features for team input.
  • Visual charts and graphs for analysis results.
  • Export options for presentations and reports.

Example:

Strengths: Strong brand reputation, skilled workforce.
Weaknesses: Limited market presence, high operational costs.
Opportunities: Emerging markets, technological advancements.
Threats: Competitive pressure, regulatory changes.

b. Creately

Description: Creately is a versatile diagramming tool that supports SWOT analysis among other strategic frameworks. It offers a drag-and-drop interface for creating detailed SWOT diagrams.

Features:

  • Drag-and-drop interface for easy diagram creation.
  • Predefined templates for SWOT analysis.
  • Real-time collaboration and sharing.
  • Integration with other productivity tools.

  1. Porter's Five Forces Analysis Tools

a. Porterâ€™s Five Forces Template by Smartsheet

Description: Smartsheet offers a template specifically designed for conducting Porter's Five Forces analysis. It helps in evaluating the competitive forces within an industry.

Features:

  • Predefined template for Porter's Five Forces analysis.
  • Customizable fields for each force.
  • Visual representation of competitive forces.
  • Collaboration and sharing features.

Example:

Competitive Rivalry: Number of competitors, industry growth.
Threat of New Entrants: Barriers to entry, brand loyalty.
Bargaining Power of Suppliers: Number of suppliers, uniqueness of service.
Bargaining Power of Buyers: Number of customers, price sensitivity.
Threat of Substitutes: Availability of substitute products, switching costs.

b. Canvanizer

Description: Canvanizer is an online tool that supports various strategic frameworks, including Porter's Five Forces. It provides a collaborative platform for teams to brainstorm and analyze competitive forces.

Features:

  • Interactive canvas for Porter's Five Forces analysis.
  • Real-time collaboration and brainstorming.
  • Customizable templates and fields.
  • Export options for analysis results.

  1. Strategy Monitoring and Control Tools

a. Balanced Scorecard by ClearPoint Strategy

Description: ClearPoint Strategy offers a comprehensive tool for implementing and monitoring the Balanced Scorecard framework. It helps in aligning business activities with strategic objectives and monitoring performance.

Features:

  • Predefined templates for Balanced Scorecard.
  • KPI tracking and performance dashboards.
  • Integration with other business tools.
  • Reporting and analytics features.

b. Tableau

Description: Tableau is a powerful data visualization tool that can be used for monitoring and controlling strategic initiatives. It helps in creating interactive dashboards and reports to track key performance indicators (KPIs).

Features:

  • Interactive data visualization and dashboards.
  • Real-time data integration and analysis.
  • Customizable reports and charts.
  • Collaboration and sharing features.

  1. Additional Resources

a. Strategy Tools by Harvard Business Review

Description: Harvard Business Review offers a collection of strategy tools and frameworks, including templates and guides for various strategic analyses.

Features:

  • Comprehensive library of strategy tools.
  • Educational articles and case studies.
  • Templates for strategic analysis.
  • Expert insights and best practices.

b. Strategic Planning Software by OnStrategy

Description: OnStrategy provides a complete strategic planning software solution that covers all aspects of strategic analysis, formulation, implementation, and monitoring.

Features:

  • End-to-end strategic planning tools.
  • Customizable templates and frameworks.
  • KPI tracking and performance management.
  • Collaboration and reporting features.

Conclusion

Utilizing software and tools for strategic analysis can significantly enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of your strategic planning process. By leveraging these tools, you can conduct comprehensive analyses, formulate robust strategies, and monitor their implementation with greater accuracy. As you explore these tools, consider your organization's specific needs and choose the ones that best align with your strategic objectives.
