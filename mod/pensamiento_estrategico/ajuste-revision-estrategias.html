Introduction

The adjustment and review of strategies is a crucial part of strategic thinking. This process involves continuously evaluating the implemented strategies to ensure they remain effective and aligned with organizational objectives. In this section, we will learn how to adjust and review strategies effectively.

Importance of Adjustment and Review of Strategies

  • Adaptability: Allows organizations to adapt to changes in the environment.
  • Continuous Improvement: Facilitates the identification of areas for improvement.
  • Alignment: Ensures that strategies remain aligned with the organization's objectives and values.
  • Risk Mitigation: Helps identify and mitigate potential risks.

Process of Adjustment and Review of Strategies

1. Performance Evaluation

  • Key Performance Indicators (KPIs): Use KPIs to measure the success of strategies.
  • Results Analysis: Compare the obtained results with the established objectives.
  • Stakeholder Feedback: Gather opinions and suggestions from interested parties.

2. Identification of Deviations

  • Gap Analysis: Identify differences between expected and actual results.
  • Root Causes: Investigate the underlying causes of deviations.

3. Strategy Adjustment

  • Objective Review: Reevaluate and adjust objectives if necessary.
  • Tactic Modification: Change specific tactics or actions to improve performance.
  • Resource Allocation: Redistribute resources to support new tactics.

4. Implementation of Changes

  • Action Plan: Develop a detailed plan to implement changes.
  • Communication: Ensure all team members are informed and aligned with the changes.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Supervise the implementation and make adjustments as necessary.

Tools and Techniques

  • SWOT Analysis: Evaluate strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats.
  • Balanced Scorecard: Use a balanced scorecard to monitor performance.
  • Benchmarking: Compare performance with that of other leading organizations in the industry.

Practical Example

Suppose a software company has implemented a strategy to increase customer satisfaction by improving technical support. After six months, the KPIs show that customer satisfaction has only marginally improved.

Performance Evaluation

  • KPI: Customer satisfaction level (measured through surveys).
  • Result: 5% increase in customer satisfaction, the goal was 20%.

Identification of Deviations

  • Gap Analysis: The gap is 15%.
  • Root Causes: Identification that response time is still high.

Strategy Adjustment

  • Objective Review: Maintain the goal of a 20% increase.
  • Tactic Modification: Implement a more efficient ticketing system and train staff in quick problem resolution.
  • Resource Allocation: Assign more staff to the technical support team.

Implementation of Changes

  • Action Plan: Develop a schedule for implementing the new ticketing system and training.
  • Communication: Inform the support team about the changes and the schedule.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Review KPIs monthly to evaluate the impact of changes.

Conclusion

The adjustment and review of strategies is a continuous and dynamic process that allows organizations to stay competitive and aligned with their objectives. By using appropriate tools and techniques and following a structured process, organizations can improve their performance and adapt to environmental changes.
