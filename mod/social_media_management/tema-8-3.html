Introduction

In this section, we will learn how to present and evaluate the performance of our social media management strategies. Presentation and evaluation are crucial to understanding the impact of our actions and adjusting our strategies based on the results obtained.

Presentation of Results

The presentation of results is an essential part of the work of a social media manager. It is important to communicate achievements and areas for improvement to stakeholders clearly and effectively.

Key Elements in the Presentation

  • Objectives and KPIs: Start with a reminder of the objectives and KPIs set at the beginning of the campaign.
  • Quantitative Data: Include metrics such as reach, engagement, follower growth, and conversions.
  • Qualitative Data: Analyze comments, mentions, and any relevant qualitative feedback.
  • Data Visualization: Use charts and tables to make the data more understandable.

Presentation Example

# Social Media Performance Report

## Objectives and KPIs
- **Objective 1**: Increase reach by 20%
- **KPI 1**: Monthly reach

## Quantitative Data
| Metric           | Current Value | Goal       | Difference |
|------------------|---------------|------------|------------|
| Reach            | 25,000        | 20,000     | +5,000     |
| Engagements      | 3,000         | 2,500      | +500       |
| New Followers    | 1,200         | 1,000      | +200       |

## Qualitative Data
- **Positive Comments**: 150
- **Negative Comments**: 20
- **Mentions**: 50

Strategy Evaluation

Evaluating social media strategies is fundamental to understanding what is working and what is not. This allows for adjustments and continuous improvement.

Steps for Evaluation

  1. Review Objectives and KPIs: Check if the objectives and KPIs have been met.
  2. Metric Analysis: Examine key metrics to identify trends and patterns.
  3. Audience Feedback: Consider feedback received from followers and customers.
  4. Competitor Comparison: Analyze how your results compare with those of the competition.
  5. Results Report: Document findings and provide recommendations.

Evaluation Example

# Social Media Strategy Evaluation

## Review of Objectives and KPIs
- **Objective 1**: Increase reach by 20% - **Achieved**
- **Objective 2**: Increase engagements by 15% - **Achieved**

## Metric Analysis
- **Reach**: Increased by 25%
- **Engagements**: Increased by 20%
- **New Followers**: Increased by 12%

## Audience Feedback
- **Positive Comments**: Majority of positive comments about visual content.
- **Negative Comments**: Some negative comments about posting frequency.

## Competitor Comparison
- **Competitor A**: Similar reach but lower engagement.
- **Competitor B**: Higher number of followers but lower monthly growth.

## Results Report
- **Recommendations**:
  - Maintain the current posting frequency.
  - Increase the use of visual content.
  - Explore new platforms to increase reach.

Conclusion

Presentation and evaluation are essential components in social media management. A clear and well-structured presentation of results allows stakeholders to understand the impact of implemented strategies. Continuous evaluation of these strategies ensures that necessary adjustments can be made to improve performance and achieve established objectives.
