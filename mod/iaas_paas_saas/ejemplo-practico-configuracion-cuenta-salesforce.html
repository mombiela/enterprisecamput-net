Introduction

In this section, we will learn how to set up an account in Salesforce, a widely used SaaS (Software as a Service) platform for customer relationship management (CRM). This practical exercise will help you understand how to interact with a SaaS platform and leverage its functionalities without needing to manage the underlying infrastructure.

Step 1: Register on Salesforce

To start using Salesforce, you first need to create an account. Follow these steps:

  1. Visit the Salesforce website: Open your browser and go to Salesforce.
  2. Select the free trial option: On the main page, look for and click on the "Try for Free" or "Prueba gratuita" button.
  3. Complete the registration form:
    • First Name: Enter your first name.
    • Last Name: Enter your last name.
    • Email: Provide a valid email address.
    • Company Name: Enter your company name or "Individual" if you are not associated with any company.
    • Phone Number: Provide a contact number.
    • Country: Select your country of residence.
    • Accept the terms and conditions: Check the corresponding box.
  4. Click on "Start my free trial": Once the form is completed, click on the button to start your free trial.

Step 2: Initial Setup

After registering, you will receive a confirmation email. Follow these steps to complete the initial setup:

  1. Verify your email: Open the confirmation email and click on the provided link to verify your email address.
  2. Create a password: Enter a secure password for your Salesforce account.
  3. Set up security questions: Select and answer security questions to protect your account.
  4. Access the Salesforce dashboard: Once the setup is complete, you will be redirected to the main Salesforce dashboard.

Step 3: Basic Navigation in Salesforce

Familiarize yourself with the Salesforce user interface:

  • Navigation bar: Located at the top, it allows access to different applications and objects like Accounts, Contacts, Opportunities, etc.
  • Home panel: Provides an overview of your activities and key metrics.
  • Settings: Access the settings options by clicking on the gear icon in the upper right corner.

Step 4: Creating an Account

Now that you are familiar with the interface, let's create an account in Salesforce:

  1. Access the Accounts section: In the navigation bar, click on "Accounts".
  2. Create a new account:
    • Click on the "New" button.
    • Complete the required fields:
      • Account Name: Enter the name of the company or client.
      • Account Type: Select the type of account (Customer, Supplier, etc.).
      • Industry: Select the corresponding industry.
      • Phone Number: Provide a contact number.
      • Address: Enter the physical address of the account.
  3. Save the account: Click on the "Save" button to store the information.

Code Example

Although Salesforce is a SaaS platform and does not require programming for basic configurations, you can use Apex (Salesforce's programming language) to automate advanced tasks. Here is a simple example of an Apex trigger to update the "Description" field of an account when it is created:

trigger UpdateAccountDescription on Account (before insert) {
    for (Account acc : Trigger.new) {
        acc.Description = 'Account created on ' + Date.today();
    }
}

Conclusion

In this practical exercise, we have learned how to set up an account in Salesforce, from the initial registration to creating an account within the platform. This process illustrates how SaaS solutions can simplify customer relationship management without the need to manage the underlying infrastructure. Salesforce offers a wide range of functionalities that you can explore to optimize your business processes.

Remember that constant practice and exploration of Salesforce's different features will allow you to make the most of this powerful tool.
