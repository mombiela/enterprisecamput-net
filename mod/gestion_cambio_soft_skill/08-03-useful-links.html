Useful Links

In this section, we provide a curated list of useful links that can help you deepen your understanding of change management, particularly in the context of technological change. These resources include articles, tools, frameworks, and communities that are valuable for both beginners and experienced professionals.

Articles and Blogs

  1. Harvard Business Review - Change Management: HBR Change Management

    • A collection of articles and insights on change management from one of the leading business publications.

  2. McKinsey & Company - Change Management: McKinsey Change Management

    • In-depth articles and case studies on change management strategies and best practices.

  3. Prosci Blog: Prosci Blog

    • Regularly updated blog with articles on change management methodologies, tools, and case studies.

Frameworks and Methodologies

  1. ADKAR Model: Prosci ADKAR Model

    • A goal-oriented change management model that guides individual and organizational change.

  2. Kotter’s 8-Step Change Model: Kotter’s 8-Step Model

    • A comprehensive framework for leading change in organizations, developed by John Kotter.

  3. Lewin’s Change Management Model: Lewin’s Model

    • A simple and practical model for understanding and managing change.

Tools and Software

  1. ChangeGear: ChangeGear

    • A change management software that helps organizations manage IT changes effectively.

  2. ServiceNow: ServiceNow Change Management

    • A comprehensive IT service management tool that includes robust change management capabilities.

  3. Jira Service Management: Jira Service Management

    • A tool that helps IT teams manage change requests and track changes efficiently.

Online Courses and Certifications

  1. Coursera - Change Management: Coursera Change Management

    • A variety of online courses on change management from top universities and institutions.

  2. LinkedIn Learning - Change Management: LinkedIn Learning Change Management

    • A wide range of video courses on change management, including practical tips and strategies.

  3. Prosci Change Management Certification: Prosci Certification

    • A certification program that provides in-depth training on the Prosci ADKAR Model and other change management tools.

Communities and Forums

  1. Change Management Institute: Change Management Institute

    • A global professional association of change managers that provides resources, events, and networking opportunities.

  2. Project Management Institute (PMI) - Change Management Community of Practice: PMI Change Management

    • A community within PMI focused on change management, offering resources and discussions.

  3. Reddit - Change Management: Reddit Change Management

    • An online forum where professionals discuss change management topics, share experiences, and seek advice.

Government and Industry Reports

  1. Gartner - Change Management: Gartner Change Management

    • Research and reports on change management trends and best practices from a leading research and advisory company.

  2. Forrester - Change Management: Forrester Change Management

    • Insights and reports on change management from a global market research company.

  3. Government of Canada - Change Management: Government of Canada Change Management

    • Resources and guidelines on change management practices within the Canadian public service.

Conclusion

These links provide a wealth of information and tools to help you navigate the complexities of change management in your organization. Whether you are looking for theoretical frameworks, practical tools, or community support, these resources will be invaluable in your journey to effectively manage technological change.
