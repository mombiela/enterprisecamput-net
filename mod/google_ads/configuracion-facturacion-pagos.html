Introduction

In this section of the course, you will learn how to set up billing and payments in Google Ads. This is a crucial step to ensure that your advertising campaigns run smoothly and that you can manage your expenses effectively.

Billing Configuration

Create a Billing Account

To start using Google Ads, you first need to create a billing account. Follow these steps:

  1. Access your Google Ads account.
  2. Click on the tools icon in the upper right corner and select "Billing and payments."
  3. Click on "Billing setup."
  4. Enter your company information, including name, address, and tax identification number.
  5. Select your preferred payment method (credit card, debit, bank transfer, etc.).
  6. Review and confirm your information to complete the setup.

Available Payment Methods

Google Ads offers several payment methods to suit the needs of different businesses:

  • Credit or debit card: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, etc.
  • Bank transfer: Ideal for large companies that prefer to handle payments through their banks.
  • Automatic debit: Allows Google Ads to automatically debit funds from your bank account.
  • PayPal: Available in some countries as an additional option.

Automatic and Manual Payment Setup

Google Ads allows two types of payments:

  • Automatic payments: Google Ads charges automatically when you reach a billing threshold or at the end of the monthly billing cycle.
  • Manual payments: You decide when to add funds to your Google Ads account. This gives you more control over your budget.

Billing Adjustments

You can make adjustments to your billing settings at any time:

  • Update payment information: Change or add payment methods as needed.

Practical Example

Below is an example of how to set up a payment method using the Google Ads API:

from google.ads.google_ads.client import GoogleAdsClient
from google.ads.google_ads.errors import GoogleAdsException

def add_payment_method(client, customer_id, payment_method):
    try:
        # Create a billing service client
        billing_service = client.get_service("BillingSetupService")

        # Set up the payment method
        billing_setup_operation = client.get_type("BillingSetupOperation")
        billing_setup = billing_setup_operation.create
        billing_setup.customer = client.get_service("CustomerService").customer_path(customer_id)
        billing_setup.payments_account = payment_method

        # Send the request to add the payment method
        response = billing_service.mutate_billing_setup(customer_id, billing_setup_operation)
        print(f"Payment method added with resource name: {response.result.resource_name}")
    except GoogleAdsException as ex:
        print(f"An error occurred: {ex}")

# Example usage
client = GoogleAdsClient.load_from_storage("path/to/google-ads.yaml")
add_payment_method(client, "123-456-7890", "INSERT_PAYMENT_METHOD_ID_HERE")
