Introduction

The final exam for this course on Facebook Ads is designed to assess your understanding and skills in creating, managing, and optimizing advertising campaigns on Facebook. Throughout the exam, you will be asked to apply the concepts and techniques learned during the course to solve practical and theoretical problems.

Exam Sections

1. Account Setup and Campaign Objectives

  • Key Concepts:

    • Creating a Facebook advertising account.
    • Setting up payment methods.
    • Selecting campaign objectives (conversion, traffic, engagement, etc.).

  • Sample Question:

    Describe the necessary steps to set up a Facebook advertising account and select an appropriate campaign objective for an online store that wants to increase sales.

2. Audience Targeting

  • Key Concepts:

    • Defining custom and lookalike audiences.
    • Using demographics, interests, and behaviors to segment audiences.
    • Implementing Facebook pixels for conversion tracking.

  • Sample Question:

    How would you create a custom audience based on visitors to your website in the last 30 days? Provide detailed steps.

3. Ad Creation

  • Key Concepts:

    • Types of ads (image, video, carousel, collection).
    • Technical specifications for each type of ad.
    • Writing effective ad copy.

  • Sample Question:

    Write an example of ad copy for a video ad promoting a new tech product. Include a clear call to action.

4. Budget and Bidding

  • Key Concepts:

    • Differences between daily budget and total budget.
    • Bidding strategies (cost per click, cost per thousand impressions, cost per action).
    • Optimizing the budget to maximize return on investment (ROI).

  • Sample Question:

    Explain how you would adjust your bidding strategy if you notice that your cost per conversion is too high. What specific changes would you make?

5. Analysis and Optimization

  • Key Concepts:

    • Using Facebook Ads Manager for performance analysis.
    • Key metrics (CTR, CPC, CPA, ROAS).
    • Campaign optimization techniques (A/B testing, audience adjustments, creative changes).

  • Sample Question:

    An ad has a low CTR but a high CPC. What metrics would you analyze and what changes would you implement to improve the ad's performance?

Conclusion

The final exam is an opportunity to demonstrate your mastery of Facebook Ads and your ability to apply the knowledge acquired in practical situations. Be sure to review all key concepts and practice with real examples to be well prepared. Good luck!
