In this section of the course on Google Data Studio, we will explore the various options available for sharing reports. You will learn how to collaborate with other users, control access permissions, and use shared links to distribute your reports effectively.

Sharing Options

Google Data Studio offers several ways to share your reports with other users. Here are the main options:

  • Share with specific users: You can invite specific people to view or edit your report.
  • Shared link: Generate a link that you can share with anyone, allowing them to view or edit the report according to the permissions you set.
  • Embed reports: Insert your reports into websites or blogs using HTML code.
  • Download as PDF: Export your report to a PDF file to share it outside of Google Data Studio.

Share with Specific Users

To share a report with specific users, follow these steps:

  1. Open the report you want to share.
  2. Click the "Share" button in the upper right corner.
  3. Enter the email addresses of the people you want to share the report with.
  4. Select the appropriate permissions (view or edit).
  5. Click "Send".

Example

1. Open the "Monthly Sales" report.
2. Click "Share".
3. Enter the email "example@domain.com".
4. Select "Can view".
5. Click "Send".

Shared Link

To generate a shared link:

  1. Open the report.
  2. Click "Share".
  3. In the "Get link" section, click "Copy link".
  4. Set the link permissions (view or edit).
  5. Share the link with the desired people.

Example

1. Open the "Web Traffic Analysis" report.
2. Click "Share".
3. In "Get link", click "Copy link".
4. Set the permissions to "Anyone with the link can view".
5. Share the link: https://datastudio.google.com/reporting/example

Embed Reports

To embed a report on a website:

  1. Open the report.
  2. Click "File" > "Embed report".
  3. Copy the provided HTML code.
  4. Paste the code into the HTML of your website or blog.

Example

<iframe width="600" height="450" src="https://datastudio.google.com/reporting/example" frameborder="0" style="border:0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Download as PDF

To download a report as a PDF:

  1. Open the report.
  2. Click "File" > "Download as PDF".
  3. Set the download options (pages, orientation, etc.).
  4. Click "Download".

Example

1. Open the "Financial Summary" report.
2. Click "File" > "Download as PDF".
3. Select "All pages".
4. Click "Download".

Comparison of Options

| Option | Available Permissions | Ideal For | |----------------------|-----------------------|----------------------------------------------| | Specific Users | View, Edit | Direct collaboration with team members | | Shared Link | View, Edit | Broad distribution without needing accounts | | Embed Reports | View | Publishing on websites or blogs | | Download as PDF | N/A | Sharing outside of Google Data Studio |

Conclusion

Sharing reports in Google Data Studio is a powerful feature that facilitates collaboration and data distribution. Whether you need to collaborate with colleagues, distribute reports to a wider audience, or embed your data on a website, Google Data Studio offers flexible options to meet your needs. Be sure to set permissions appropriately to maintain control over who can view or edit your reports.
