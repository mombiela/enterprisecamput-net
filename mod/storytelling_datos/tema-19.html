Introduction

Case 1: Improving Customer Service in a Telecommunications Company

Problem Description

A telecommunications company was facing issues with customer satisfaction due to prolonged wait times and ineffective problem resolution.

Data Analysis

  • Data Collection: Data from customer service calls were collected, including wait times, call durations, and satisfaction survey results.
  • Data Visualization: Line charts were used to show the trend of wait times and bar charts to compare customer satisfaction before and after implementing changes.

Implemented Solutions

  • Resource Optimization: Redistribution of staff during peak hours.
  • Training: Training programs to improve agent efficiency.

Results

  • Reduction in Wait Time: 30% decrease in wait times.
  • Increase in Customer Satisfaction: 20% improvement in satisfaction surveys.
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt

# Sample data
wait_times = [10, 8, 7, 5, 4]
customer_satisfaction = [70, 75, 80, 85, 90]
months = ['January', 'February', 'March', 'April', 'May']

# Line chart for wait times
plt.figure(figsize=(10, 5))
plt.plot(months, wait_times, marker='o', label='Wait Time (min)')
plt.xlabel('Months')
plt.ylabel('Wait Time (min)')
plt.title('Wait Times in Customer Service Center')
plt.legend()
plt.show()

# Bar chart for customer satisfaction
plt.figure(figsize=(10, 5))
plt.bar(months, customer_satisfaction, color='skyblue', label='Customer Satisfaction (%)')
plt.xlabel('Months')
plt.ylabel('Customer Satisfaction (%)')
plt.title('Customer Satisfaction Over Time')
plt.legend()
plt.show()

Case 2: Inventory Optimization in a Supermarket Chain

Problem Description

A supermarket chain was facing issues with excess inventory and perishable products going to waste.

Data Analysis

  • Data Collection: Daily sales data, product expiration dates, and inventory levels.
  • Data Visualization: Scatter plots to identify sales patterns and bar charts to show product waste.

Implemented Solutions

  • Demand Forecasting: Predictive models to adjust inventory levels.
  • Inventory Management: Implementation of a product rotation system based on expiration dates.

Results

  • Reduction in Waste: 25% decrease in perishable product waste.
  • Inventory Optimization: 15% reduction in storage costs.
import pandas as pd
import seaborn as sns

# Sample data
data = {
    'Days': [1, 2, 3, 4, 5],
    'Sales': [100, 150, 200, 250, 300],
    'Waste': [10, 8, 6, 4, 2]
}
df = pd.DataFrame(data)

# Scatter plot for sales
sns.scatterplot(x='Days', y='Sales', data=df)
plt.title('Daily Sales Patterns')
plt.xlabel('Days')
plt.ylabel('Sales')
plt.show()

# Bar chart for waste
sns.barplot(x='Days', y='Waste', data=df, palette='viridis')
plt.title('Perishable Product Waste')
plt.xlabel('Days')
plt.ylabel('Waste')
plt.show()
