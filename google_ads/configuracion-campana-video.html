Introduction

In this section, you will learn how to set up a video campaign in Google Ads. Video campaigns are an excellent way to promote your brand, products, or services through ads on YouTube and other websites in the Google Display Network.

Steps to Set Up a Video Campaign

1. Create a New Campaign

To get started, follow these steps:

  • Log in to your Google Ads account.
  • Click the "+" button and select "New campaign."
  • Choose the goal of your campaign (e.g., "Sales," "Leads," "Website traffic," etc.).
  • Select "Video" as the campaign type.

2. Configure Campaign Details

In this section, you will configure the basic details of your campaign:

  • Campaign name: Assign a descriptive name to your campaign.
  • Bidding strategy: Select the bidding strategy that best suits your goals (e.g., Maximum CPV, Target CPA).
  • Budget and dates: Define the daily or total budget and the start and end dates of the campaign.

3. Set Up Targeting

Targeting is crucial to ensure your ads reach the right audience:

  • Locations: Select the geographic locations where you want your ads to be shown.
  • Languages: Choose the languages your audience speaks.
  • Demographics: Define the age, gender, parental status, and income level of your audience.
  • Audiences: Select specific audiences based on interests, behaviors, or demographics.

4. Create the Ad Group

Within your campaign, you can have multiple ad groups. Set up your first ad group:

  • Ad group name: Assign a descriptive name.
  • Bid: Define the maximum bid you are willing to pay per view (CPV) or per thousand impressions (CPM).

5. Create the Video Ad

Now, it's time to create your video ad:

  • Video URL: Enter the URL of the YouTube video you want to promote.
  • Ad format: Select the ad format (e.g., TrueView in-stream, TrueView discovery).
  • Final URL: Enter the destination URL where you want to direct users who click on your ad.
  • Call to action (CTA): Add a clear and attractive CTA.
  • Title and description: Provide a title and description that capture your audience's attention.

Example of Video Ad Setup

Ad group name: Summer Promotion
Maximum CPV bid: $0.10
Video URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=example
Ad format: TrueView in-stream
Final URL: https://www.yoursite.com/summer-promotion
Call to action: Buy now!
Title: Summer Offer
Description: Take advantage of our exclusive summer offers.

Conclusion

Setting up a video campaign in Google Ads may seem complex at first, but by following these steps, you can create effective campaigns that reach your target audience. Always remember to monitor and adjust your campaigns to optimize their performance.
