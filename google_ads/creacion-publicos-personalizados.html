In this section of the Google Ads course, you will learn how to create custom audiences for your campaigns. Custom audiences allow you to segment your ads more precisely, reaching people who are more likely to be interested in your products or services.

What are Custom Audiences?

Custom audiences are audience segments that you can define based on specific data about your customers and website visitors. This data can include:

  • Website behavior
  • Demographic data
  • Interests and affinities
  • Customer lists

Types of Custom Audiences

There are several types of custom audiences you can create in Google Ads:

  1. Custom audiences based on website data:

    • Users who visited specific pages.
    • Users who performed specific actions (such as completing a form).
    • Users who spent a certain amount of time on your site.

  2. Custom audiences based on customer lists:

    • Email lists.
    • Phone numbers.
    • Physical addresses.

  3. Custom audiences based on mobile apps:

    • Users who installed your app.
    • Users who performed specific actions within the app.

  4. Custom audiences based on YouTube:

    • Users who watched your videos.
    • Users who interacted with your channel.

Creating Custom Audiences in Google Ads

Step 1: Access the Audiences Section

  1. Log in to your Google Ads account.
  2. In the left menu, select "Tools and settings".
  3. In the "Shared library" section, select "Audience manager".

Step 2: Create a New Custom Audience

  1. Click the "+" button to create a new audience.
  2. Select the type of audience you want to create (for example, "Custom audience based on website data").

Step 3: Set Up Audience Criteria

  1. Define the criteria for your audience. For example, if you are creating an audience based on website data, you can specify:

    • URL of the visited pages.
    • Duration of the visit.
    • Specific actions performed.

  2. Assign a name to your audience for easy identification.

Example of Setting Up an Audience Based on Website Data

Suppose you want to create an audience of users who visited the "Pricing" page of your website and spent more than 2 minutes on it.

1. Select "Custom audience based on website data".
2. In the "URL contains" section, enter "pricing".
3. In the "Duration of the visit" section, select "More than" and then enter "2 minutes".
4. Assign a name to your audience, for example, "Pricing page visitors - 2 min".

Step 4: Save and Use the Audience

  1. Click "Save" to create your custom audience.
  2. You can now use this audience in your Google Ads campaigns to segment your ads more effectively.

Conclusion

Creating custom audiences in Google Ads is a powerful tool to improve the targeting of your advertising campaigns. By defining audiences based on specific behaviors, demographic data, and customer lists, you can ensure that your ads reach the most relevant people. Practice creating different types of custom audiences to see which works best for your marketing goals.
