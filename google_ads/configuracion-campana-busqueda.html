Introduction

In this section, you will learn how to set up a search campaign in Google Ads. This type of campaign is essential to appear in Google's search results when users search for products or services related to your business.

Steps to Set Up a Search Campaign

1. Create a New Campaign

To get started, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Google Ads account.
  2. Click on the "New Campaign" button.
  3. Select the goal of your campaign (e.g., sales, leads, website traffic).
  4. Choose the "Search Network" campaign type.

2. General Campaign Settings

In this section, you will configure the basic aspects of your campaign:

  • Campaign Name: Assign a descriptive name to easily identify your campaign.
  • Networks: Select "Google Search Network" and optionally "Google Display Network" if you want to extend the reach.
  • Locations: Define the geographic locations where you want your ads to be shown.
  • Languages: Select the languages spoken by your potential customers.

3. Budget and Bidding Settings

  • Daily Budget: Set how much you are willing to spend per day on this campaign.
  • Bidding Strategy: Choose an appropriate bidding strategy. Some common options include:
    • Manual CPC: You control the maximum cost per click.
    • Maximize Clicks: Google adjusts bids to get the most clicks possible within your budget.
    • Target CPA: Google adjusts bids to get the most conversions possible at the target cost per action.

4. Ad Extensions Settings

Ad extensions enhance the visibility and effectiveness of your ads. Some common extensions include:

  • Sitelink Extensions: Add additional links to your website.
  • Call Extensions: Allow users to call your business directly.
  • Callout Extensions: Add extra text to highlight features or benefits.

5. Creating Ad Groups

Ad groups allow you to organize your ads and keywords into specific themes:

  • Ad Group Name: Assign a descriptive name.
  • Keywords: Select relevant keywords for your ad group. Use Google's Keyword Planner to find ideas.
  • Ads: Create attractive and relevant ads for your keywords. A typical ad includes:
    • Headline 1, 2, and 3: Capture the user's attention.
    • Description 1 and 2: Provide more details about your offer.
    • Visible URL: Display the URL of your website.

Example Ad

Headline 1: Buy Sports Shoes
Headline 2: Free Shipping on Orders > $50
Headline 3: 20% Discount Today
Description 1: Wide selection of sports shoes from top brands.
Description 2: Buy now and receive your order in 24 hours.
Visible URL: www.example.com/sports-shoes

Conclusion

Setting up a search campaign in Google Ads is a process that involves several steps, from creating the campaign to configuring ad groups and keywords. By following these steps, you can create effective campaigns that attract your potential customers and improve your online business performance. Always remember to monitor and adjust your campaigns to optimize results.
