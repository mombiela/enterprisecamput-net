What would I like? I would like the AI to be just a starting point, a help to kick off a course, and from there someone would take care of completing the contents, reviewing it, etc.

And in return for what? Well, one option is advertising. For example, it could be an academy that wants to have its logo on the course, and that people see it. In return for placing the link, the academy would maintain the course content. Who says academy says IT consultant, university or anyone who has the knowledge (and availability) to do it.
