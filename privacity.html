At EnterpriseCampus, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information provided by users registered on our platform.

  1. Information We Collect

During the registration process and through use of our platform, we collect the following personal information:

  • Contact information: name, email address, and any other information the user voluntarily provides.
  • Progress data: tracking of completed courses, progress in each course, and learning-related statistics.

This data is used exclusively to personalize and enhance the educational experience on our platform.

  1. Use of Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Monitor the user's progress in courses and provide personal statistics.
  • Enhance the learning experience by tailoring content according to the userâ€™s progress and performance.

We do not share personal information with third parties except when required by law or with the explicit consent of the user.

  1. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect users' personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, due to the nature of the Internet, we cannot guarantee absolute data security.

  1. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdWords and Google Analytics to obtain statistical data on the use of our platform in an anonymous manner. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

  1. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access, rectify, or delete their personal information.
  • Request restriction of the processing of their personal data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time for data processing as permitted by law.

Users can exercise these rights by:

  • Contacting us at admin@campusempresa.cat.
  • Managing their personal information directly from their profile on the website, where they can also permanently delete all personal data.

  1. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be communicated to users through a notice on the website. The last updated date will be indicated at the end of this document.

  1. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at admin@campusempresa.cat.
