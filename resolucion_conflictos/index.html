Topic Details

Definition and Types of Conflicts

Explanation of what a conflict is, the different types of conflicts that can arise in the workplace, and practical examples.

Importance of Conflict Resolution at Work

Discussion on why it is crucial to resolve conflicts effectively and the benefits for the team and the organization.

Basic Principles of Conflict Resolution

Introduction to the fundamental principles that guide conflict resolution, such as impartiality, confidentiality, and mutual respect.

Effective Communication

Techniques and practices to improve communication in conflict situations, including the use of clear and constructive language.

Active Listening

Methods to develop the skill of active listening, which includes paying attention, showing interest, and responding appropriately.

Empathy and Understanding

How to develop empathy and understanding to see the perspective of the other parties involved in the conflict.

Negotiation and Mediation

Strategies to negotiate and mediate effectively, seeking solutions that benefit all parties.

Problem Identification and Cause Analysis

Tools and techniques to identify underlying problems and analyze the root causes of conflicts.

Mediation Techniques

Specific mediation methods that can be applied in different types of workplace conflicts.

Negotiation Strategies

Advanced negotiation strategies to reach satisfactory and lasting agreements.

Collaborative Problem Solving

Collaborative approaches to problem-solving that foster cooperation and teamwork.

Case Studies

Analysis of real and fictional cases to apply the acquired knowledge and discuss possible solutions.

Role-Playing and Simulations

Practical role-playing and simulation exercises to practice conflict resolution in a controlled environment.

Evaluation and Feedback

Methods to evaluate performance in conflict resolution and provide constructive feedback.

Conflict Prevention

Strategies to prevent conflicts before they arise, including the creation of clear policies and procedures.

Promoting a Culture of Peace

How to foster an organizational culture that values peace and constructive conflict resolution.

Continuous Skill Development

Resources and strategies for the continuous development of conflict resolution skills.

Theoretical Exam

Written evaluation to measure the theoretical knowledge acquired during the course.

Practical Evaluation

Practical evaluation to measure the ability to apply conflict resolution skills in simulated situations.

Certificate Issuance

Process of issuing certificates to participants who have successfully completed the course.
