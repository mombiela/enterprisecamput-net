A bit of history: Python in Catalan

This started as an attempt to use Artificial Intelligence to create a Python course. I wanted to learn this language and I thought... what if I try to make a Python course in Catalan with AI? Everything we see is always in English, and at most in Spanish. I tried it and it turned out pretty well.

But it wasn't enough. It had to be accessible like a real course, not just a chat prompt. And it had to be fast. And of course, without consuming AI resources every time I wanted to review a topic.

Semantic Text

Let's make a note here. 10 years ago I created a new language:

  • STxT: Semantic Text

This language was very simple and I believed (still believe) that it could be a substitute for XML, YAML, and others. And so it was forgotten for 10 years.

This year I revived it and made modifications, resulting in version 2.0; without anyone ever seeing 1.0 :-D

Well, the fact is that everything on this portal uses this language, and I think it works quite well ;-)

The project

Well, from here everything seems simple. I use AI to create a course, I use STXT to render the content in an easy way, and I think... why just one course? What if we make many?

The future

I would like to know the future, but I don't know where it will go. Will it be like STxT and stay in a drawer for 10 years or more? Will it be successful and people will want to collaborate and push it forward?

For now, it's more of an experiment than anything else, but well... who knows what awaits us!!
