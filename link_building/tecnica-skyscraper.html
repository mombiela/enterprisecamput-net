Introduction to the Skyscraper Technique

The Skyscraper Technique is a link building strategy that focuses on creating high-quality and relevant content to attract natural links from other websites. This technique was popularized by Brian Dean of Backlinko and has proven effective in improving a website's authority and ranking in search engines.

Steps to Implement the Skyscraper Technique

1. Identify Popular Content

The first step in the Skyscraper Technique is to find content that has already proven to be popular and has obtained many links. This can be done using content analysis tools such as:

  • Ahrefs: Allows you to search for content with many backlinks.
  • BuzzSumo: Helps find content that has been widely shared on social media.
  • SEMrush: Offers backlink analysis and popular content.

2. Create Improved Content

Once the popular content is identified, the next step is to create an improved version of that content. This can include:

  • Greater depth: Adding more information and details.
  • Updating data: Including more recent and relevant data.
  • Better design: Using graphics, images, and a better structure to make the content more attractive.

3. Promote the Content

After creating the improved content, it is crucial to promote it to obtain links. Some strategies include:

  • Contacting those who linked to the original content: Informing them about your improved content and suggesting they link to it.
  • Social media outreach: Sharing the content on social platforms to attract more attention.
  • Collaborations and mentions: Working with influencers or industry experts to mention your content.

Practical Example

Step 1: Identify Popular Content

Suppose we find a popular article about "10 Best SEO Tools" that has obtained many links and shares.

Step 2: Create Improved Content

We decide to create an article titled "20 Best SEO Tools for 2023". In this article, we include:

  • Detailed description of each tool.
  • Screenshots and tutorials.
  • Expert opinions.
  • Comparisons and price tables.

Step 3: Promote the Content

  • Send emails to the sites that linked to the original article, informing them about your improved content.
  • Share on social media such as LinkedIn, Twitter, and Facebook.
  • Collaborate with influencers in the SEO field to mention your article on their blogs or social media.

Conclusion

The Skyscraper Technique is an effective link building strategy that relies on improving existing content and promoting it effectively. By following the steps of identifying popular content, creating an improved version, and promoting it, you can attract high-quality links and improve your website's authority.
