Introduction

Creating an advertising account on Facebook is the first step to start using Facebook Ads and take advantage of its capabilities to reach your target audience. In this section, you will learn how to set up an advertising account from scratch, the necessary requirements, and some tips to optimize your initial setup.

Prerequisites

Before creating an advertising account on Facebook, make sure you meet the following requirements:

  • Have a personal Facebook account.
  • Have a Facebook page for your business or project.
  • Access to a valid payment method (credit card, PayPal, etc.).

Steps to create an advertising account

1. Access the Ads Manager

The Ads Manager is the main tool for creating and managing your advertising campaigns on Facebook. Follow these steps to access it:

  1. Log in to your personal Facebook account.
  2. Go to the main Facebook page and click on the dropdown menu in the top right corner.
  3. Select "Manage ads" or visit directly Facebook Ads Manager.

2. Create an advertising account

Once in the Ads Manager, follow these steps to create your advertising account:

  1. In the left menu, click on "Business settings."
  2. In the "Accounts" section, select "Ad accounts."
  3. Click on the "Add" button and select "Create a new ad account."
  4. Complete the requested information:
    • Name of the ad account.
    • Time zone.
    • Currency.
  5. Assign permissions to the people who will manage the ad account.
  6. Click on "Create ad account."

3. Set up the payment method

To activate your ad account, you must add a valid payment method:

  1. In the Ads Manager, go to "Payment settings."
  2. Click on "Add payment method."
  3. Select your preferred payment method (credit card, PayPal, etc.).
  4. Enter the required information and save the changes.

4. Verify the ad account

In some cases, Facebook may request additional verification to ensure the authenticity of your ad account. Follow the instructions provided by Facebook to complete this process.

Tips to optimize your initial setup

  • Name of the ad account: Choose a descriptive name that allows you to easily identify the account, especially if you manage multiple accounts.
  • Time zone and currency: Set these parameters according to your location and preferences to avoid confusion in billing and ad scheduling.
  • Permissions and roles: Assign appropriate roles and permissions to the people who will manage the account to maintain proper control and avoid errors.

Example of setup

Below is an example of what the initial setup of an ad account would look like:

Name of the ad account: Summer Campaign 2023
Time zone: GMT-5 (Eastern Standard Time)
Currency: USD (United States Dollar)
Payment method: Visa credit card
Permissions: 
  - Administrator: John Doe (john.doe@example.com)
  - Analyst: Mary Smith (mary.smith@example.com)

Conclusion

Creating an advertising account on Facebook is a simple but crucial process to start using Facebook Ads effectively. Make sure to follow the mentioned steps and correctly set up your account to avoid future problems. With a well-configured ad account, you will be ready to create and manage advertising campaigns that help you achieve your marketing goals.
