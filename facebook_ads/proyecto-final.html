Project Objective

The objective of this final project is to apply all the knowledge acquired during the course to create, manage, and optimize an advertising campaign on Facebook Ads. Students must demonstrate their ability to:

  • Set up an advertising campaign from scratch.
  • Define clear and measurable objectives.
  • Properly segment the audience.
  • Create effective ads.
  • Analyze and optimize the campaign's performance.

Project Requirements

To complete this project, students must meet the following requirements:

  1. Define the Campaign Objective

    • Select an appropriate campaign objective (e.g., traffic, conversions, brand awareness).
    • Justify the choice of the objective based on business goals.

  2. Audience Segmentation

    • Define the target audience using Facebook Ads segmentation tools.
    • Include demographic details, interests, and behaviors.

  3. Ad Creation

    • Design an attractive ad that includes text, images, and/or videos.
    • Ensure the ad complies with Facebook's advertising policies.

  4. Budget and Scheduling Setup

    • Set a daily or total budget for the campaign.
    • Schedule the campaign duration.

  5. Analysis and Optimization

    • Monitor the campaign's performance using Facebook Ads Manager.
    • Make adjustments based on the data obtained to improve performance.

Example of Campaign Setup

Step 1: Define the Campaign Objective

Campaign Objective: Traffic
Justification: We want to increase the number of visits to our website to generate more leads.

Step 2: Audience Segmentation

Target Audience:
- Age: 25-45 years
- Gender: All
- Location: Spain
- Interests: Technology, Digital Marketing, Entrepreneurship
- Behaviors: Frequent online shoppers

Step 3: Ad Creation

Ad Text: "Discover the latest trends in digital marketing. Visit our website and stay updated!"
Image: [Include a relevant and attractive image]
Call to Action: "Learn More"

Step 4: Budget and Scheduling Setup

Daily Budget: 20 EUR
Campaign Duration: 30 days

Step 5: Analysis and Optimization

- Monitor key metrics such as CTR (Click-Through Rate), CPC (Cost Per Click), and conversions.
- Make adjustments to audience segmentation and ad content if necessary.
- Test different ad variations (A/B Testing) to identify the most effective version.

Conclusion

In this final project, students will have had the opportunity to practically apply the knowledge acquired about Facebook Ads. Upon completing this project, they should feel comfortable creating and managing advertising campaigns on Facebook, as well as analyzing and optimizing their performance to achieve the established business objectives.
